# TidePebble

TidePebble puts the local tide forecast on your wrist. It uses your phone’s location to find the nearest available marine forecast and displays the next 24 hours as a clear tide-height chart on your Pebble.

See the current tide level and direction at a glance, check the next high and low tides, and browse the full daily curve directly from the watch. TidePebble also shows current swell height and sea temperature, using your system measurement preferences where supported.

Prefer another spot? Open the settings page to search for a location manually or switch back to phone GPS at any time.

Forecast data is provided by Open-Meteo. TidePebble is intended for general planning and should not replace official local tide tables or safety information.
